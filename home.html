About

Since 2020

Since we opened our first office in Dublin in 2020, Travel World has been committed to bringing travellers the best value and quality travel arrangements across the world. Our team are passionate about travel and we provide corporate travellers a best in class service to facilitate their travelling needs. Our Company is one of the largest independently owned travel agency headquartered in Ireland.

Our Mission

In Travel World we are one of the most exciting, innovative and trustworthy travel companies to work for and as such we are able to attract the best talent to join our team. From our Travel Specialists, who spend at least 4 weeks of their working year exploring our destinations, to our remote team of content writers, videographers and marketers, who have the freedom to work from any place they choose.
